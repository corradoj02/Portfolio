About Me

My name is Joseph Corrado;
I mainly just go by "Joe". I am an aspiring software developer who thoroughly enjoys challenges and solving problems. I grew up around loving everything tech. I would break and dissect everything electronic/electrical in nature, then fix it or put it all back together to further my understanding of it. I was building desktops and repairing laptops at age 8, and by the time I graduated high school I was configuring and troubleshooting Cisco routers and switches. I've decided to pursue a career in software because of its technical and challenging nature. I'm using this site as a portfolio to demonstrate my past and present projects. I hope to connect and collaborate with whomever is interested and build (maybe even fix) a lot of great things!
